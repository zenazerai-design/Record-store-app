Liner Notes

The full story.

I'm Zena, a Senior Product Designer in London, currently shipping things that actually work at GWI. Seven-plus years across agencies and scale-ups. I get up to speed fast, cut through the noise, and come out the other side with something clear.

I work end-to-end, from the messy, who-even-owns-this phase all the way to production-ready handoff. Before design, I spent time in law and music PR. That combination left me with a love of rigour, a feel for a good story, and a pun habit my colleagues have learned to live with.
